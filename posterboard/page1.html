Design Constraints

Experiential Constraints

  • Support full-body interaction
  • Continuous auditory feedback
  • Respond proportionally to both subtle and large-scale movements
  • Enable fluid engagement across a wide range of motion dynamics, from slow, controlled gestures to fast, energetic actions
  • Low latency
  • Smooth parameter transitions, consistent system behavior; even minor delays or unstable audio artifacts can disrupt immersion and weaken this coupling

Technical Constraints

  • No need for specialized calibration or external infrastructure
  • End-to-end system latency < 70 ms
  • Spatial jitter in estimated body position < 3 cm to prevent audible instability
  • Motion tracking accuracy > 85% under nominal conditions
  • Audio pipeline: no buffer underruns, audible glitches, or drift-related degradation
  • Sustain stable operation for at least 15 minutes of uninterrupted use

Cost / Resource Constraints

  • Operate reliably in ordinary indoor environments (typical household lighting and spatial constraints)
  • Affordable, consumer-grade hardware
  • Widely available open source software
