Big Question

Can real-time sensing technologies be combined to create a continuous, responsive system that transforms human movement into sound?

Vision

Create an interactive medium that:

  • encourages sustained physical engagement
  • heightened enjoyment and motivation
  • improved proprioceptive awareness
  • sense of flow-like immersion

Applications: wellness, rehabilitation, dance technology, and interactive media.
